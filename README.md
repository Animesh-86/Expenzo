# Expenzo 💰 — Your AI-Powered Finance Buddy

Expenzo is a premium, high-performance personal finance manager built with Flutter. Designed for users who demand both **visual excellence** and **robust data integrity**, Expenzo helps you track, analyze, and master your money with a sleek, modern interface.

![Expenzo Logo](Expenzo/assets/images/app_icon.png)

## ✨ Premium Features

### 🎯 Smart Savings Goals
Transform your dreams into reality. Unlike traditional trackers, Expenzo provides:
*   **Target Deadlines**: Set a date for your goal (e.g., "New iPhone by Dec 25").
*   **Weekly Savings Targets**: The app automatically calculates exactly how much you need to save **per week** to reach your goal on time.
*   **Progress Tracking**: Beautiful, color-coded progress bars and percentage tracking.
*   **Categorization**: Organize goals into Travel, Tech, Education, Emergency Funds, and more.
*   **Quick Contributions**: Add savings instantly with +₹100, +₹500, or +₹1000 quick-action buttons.

### 📊 Advanced Analytics & Insights
Understand your spending patterns like never before:
*   **Dynamic Charts**: Interactive Pie charts and Line graphs showing daily, weekly, and monthly trends.
*   **Top Category Trends**: Compare this month's spending against the last to see where you're improving.
*   **Customizable Dashboard**: Hide or show charts based on your preference for a cleaner look.
*   **AI-Ready Infrastructure**: Built with hooks for deep financial analysis and future AI integrations.

### 🛡️ Unrivaled Stability (Hive Persistence)
Your data is precious. Expenzo uses a customized **Self-Healing Architecture**:
*   **Auto-Recovery**: If the local database (Hive) ever becomes corrupted, the app automatically detects it, clears the fault, and recreates the storage to prevent app crashes.
*   **Resilient Providers**: All data providers are wrapped in safety logic to ensure the UI stays responsive even during background data loads.

### 🕒 Recurring Expenses
Never forget a bill again:
*   Automate your fixed costs like Rent, Netflix, or Gym memberships.
*   Set frequencies: Daily, Weekly, Monthly, or Yearly.
*   Automatically logs the expense when the due date hits.

### 💸 Budget Management
*   Set global monthly spending limits.
*   Assign specific budgets to categories (Food, Shopping, Health, etc.).
*   Real-time progress bars show you how much "safety room" you have left.

### 📤 Export & Portability
*   **CSV Export**: Export all your transaction history to a standard CSV file.
*   **Instant Sharing**: Share your financial reports directly via Email, WhatsApp, or cloud storage.

## 🎨 Design Philosophy
Expenzo is built using **Glassmorphism** and **Dark Mode** first principles.
*   **Harmonious Colors**: No generic reds or greens—curated HSL-tailored palettes.
*   **Typography**: Clean, modern fonts (Inter/Roboto) for maximum readability.
*   **Micro-animations**: Smooth transitions and hover effects that make the app feel alive.

## 📱 Installation

### 📥 Download the APK
Get the latest stable release here:
[**Download Expenzo v1.0.0 (Release APK)**](release/expenzo_v1.0.0.apk)

### 🛠️ Developer Setup
If you want to build Expenzo from source:
1.  **Clone the Repo**: `git clone https://github.com/Animesh-86/Expenzo.git`
2.  **Install Dependencies**: `flutter pub get`
3.  **Generate Adapters**: `flutter pub run build_runner build --delete-conflicting-outputs`
4.  **Run the App**: `flutter run`
